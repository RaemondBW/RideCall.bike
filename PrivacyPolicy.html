Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

The developers of Ride Call created this privacy policy to explain how we process personal information and your privacy rights. If you have any questions, please contact us through our support page.

1. Personal Information Collection and Use

The app collects the following types of data with your permission:

  • Location: Used to provide accurate local weather data for your area
  • Notifications: Used to send evening alerts about tomorrow's ride conditions
  • Alarms: Used to schedule wake-up alarms that work even in Do Not Disturb mode

You can modify these permissions through iOS Settings at any time without permanent consequences beyond reduced functionality.

Technical device information like IP addresses may be received during normal app operation.

2. Personal Information Sharing

We do not share any personal information.

3. Social Logins

Social logins are not implemented in this app.

4. In-app Purchase Information

In-app purchase information is not processed by the application.

5. Children's Personal Information

The minimum age requirement for using Ride Call is 13 years old. We commit to deleting any inadvertently collected child data promptly.

6. Data Retention

We do not store your personal information.

7. Data Protection

No personal information collection occurs beyond what is necessary for the app to function.

8. Policy Changes

Updates to this policy will appear in the app's change list.

9. Accessibility

This website complies with WCAG 2.1 standards.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact Raemond Bergstrom-Wood via our Support Page.
